# Project BIG-ES

> **Barangay Integrated Governance and Environmental Sustainability with Revenue Projections Using Prophet Forecasting Model**

*A Business Analytics Capstone Project developed for Barangay Bagbag, Quezon City.*

---

# Overview

Project BIG-ES is a centralized web-based platform designed to modernize barangay governance by integrating digital public services, environmental sustainability, and business analytics into a single system.

The platform enables barangay officials to manage resident services, monitor Material Recovery Facility (MRF) operations, track urban farming activities, and generate revenue projections using the Prophet Forecasting Model for data-driven decision-making.

---

# Problem Statement

Many barangays still rely on manual processes for:

- Resident record management
- Barangay e-services
- Waste monitoring
- Revenue tracking
- Sustainability reporting

These manual workflows often result in delayed transactions, fragmented records, and limited visibility into financial and environmental performance.

---

# Solution

Project BIG-ES provides a centralized digital platform that combines:

- Digital Governance
- Environmental Sustainability
- Business Analytics
- Predictive Revenue Forecasting

into one integrated web application.
